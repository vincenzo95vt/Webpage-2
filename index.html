Synopsis

In wartime, the brilliant American physicist Julius Robert Oppenheimer, at the head of the 'Manhattan Project', leads nuclear tests to build the atomic bomb for his country. Shocked by its destructive power, Oppenheimer questions the moral consequences of his creation. From then on, and for the rest of his life, he would firmly oppose the use of nuclear weapons.
